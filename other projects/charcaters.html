Project Launch - 9th Grade

The Polymath

Leonardo da Vinci

Who was this person?

An Italian artist, scientist, and inventor.

Cool Fact: He designed flying machines 500 years before airplanes existed!

The Pioneer

Marie Curie

Who was this person?

A Polish scientist who studied radioactivity.

Cool Fact: She is the only person to win Nobel Prizes in two different sciences.

The Conqueror

Alexander the Great

Who was this person?

A young King of Macedonia who never lost a battle.

Cool Fact: By age 30, he created one of the largest empires in history.

The Last Pharaoh

Cleopatra

Who was this person?

The intelligent and powerful ruler of Ancient Egypt.

Cool Fact: She spoke 9 languages and was a brilliant political leader.

The Visionary

Nikola Tesla

Who was this person?

The inventor of the modern AC electricity system.

Cool Fact: He dreamed of free wireless energy for the entire world.

The Emperor

Napoleon Bonaparte

Who was this person?

A French military leader who conquered most of Europe.

Cool Fact: He changed European law forever with the Napoleonic Code.

The Warrior

Joan of Arc

Who was this person?

A teenage girl who led the French army to victory.

Cool Fact: She followed her visions and became a hero at only 17 years old.

The King

Henry VIII

Who was this person?

The King of England famous for his six wives.

Cool Fact: He broke away from the Church just to get a divorce. Dramatic!

The Queen

Elizabeth I

Who was this person?

One of the most successful queens in English history.

Cool Fact: She defeated the Spanish Armada and started a Golden Age of art.

The Dictator

Julius Caesar

Who was this person?

A general who ended the Roman Republic to become a dictator.

Cool Fact: His name became the word for 'King' in many languages (Czar, Kaiser).

The Steppe Lord

Genghis Khan

Who was this person?

Founder of the Mongol Empire.

Cool Fact: He started as an orphan and created the largest land empire ever.

The Empress

Catherine the Great

Who was this person?

The longest-ruling female leader of Russia.

Cool Fact: She was born in Germany but became the most powerful woman in Russia.

The Leader

Nelson Mandela

Who was this person?

The leader who ended Apartheid in South Africa.

Cool Fact: He was in prison for 27 years and then became President. A true inspiration.

The Programmer

Ada Lovelace

Who was this person?

A mathematician and brilliant writer.

Cool Fact: She wrote the first computer algorithm in the 1840s!

The Mystic

Grigori Rasputin

Who was this person?

A mysterious monk in the Russian court.

Cool Fact: People believed he had magical powers to heal the Prince.

The Genius

Albert Einstein

Who was this person?

The physicist who discovered the Theory of Relativity.

Cool Fact: He completely changed how we understand time and space.

The Peaceful Soul

Mahatma Gandhi

Who was this person?

The leader of the Indian independence movement.

Cool Fact: He defeated an empire using only non-violence and peace.

The Explorer

Hernán Cortés

Who was this person?

The Spanish soldier who led the fall of the Aztecs.

Cool Fact: He burned his own ships so his men couldn't retreat.

The Activist

Malala Yousafzai

Who was this person?

A young activist for female education.

Cool Fact: She survived an attack and became the youngest Nobel Prize winner ever.

The Painter

Vincent van Gogh

Who was this person?

A Dutch post-impressionist painter.

Cool Fact: He only sold one painting in his life, but today he is one of the most famous artists.
